Perform a monthly "Go Puja" (ideally on a fixed day like the monthly Purnima day) wherever you may have a Goshala to help bring auspiciousness in today's troubled world. Even if you don't have a Goshala, you can perform a Go Puja to a photo of Mother Surabhi along with the chanting of the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra.

  • Hare Krishna Hare Krishna
  • Krishna Krishna Hare Hare
  • Hare Rama Hare Rama
  • Rama Rama Hare Hare
