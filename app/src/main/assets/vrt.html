Varnasrama Research Teams (VRT)

A few of the activities that can be performed by VRT members:

Creating Awareness:
  • Weekly meetings by members to educate themselves and share on varnasrama topics.
  • Devising methods/campaigns for creating awareness for stopping cow killing.
  • Training up oneself as per one’s Varna and ashrama.
  • Setting up Varnasrama stalls for distribution of books, pamphlets, DVDs etc.
  • Setting up Cow products stall in Temple/centers. Promote use of such products.
  • Conducting seminars, talk shows, road shows for spreading awareness on topics of Varnasrama.
How to Get Involved:
  • Village or Farm camps, Visits to Goshala, Feeding cows.
  • Organize Terrace (roof top) garden/farming.
  • Setting up Govinda’s or Prasadam stall.
  • Organizing Yoga or conventional health care camps.
  • Organizing Martial Arts camps or courses.
  • Enroll and lend support and assistance to other IDVM departments.
  • Organizing/helping temple and congregation to purchase from farm.
  • Supporting new VRT formations at different places to involve more people in this mission.
  • Organize Varnasrama festivals or Varnasrama fairs.
Networking:
  • Collect data of other NGOs, religious groups, scientific or cultural group that are working in this area.
  • Build relation and team up with them for campaign, etc.
Revive Traditions:
  • Preparing for living a simple life in future by purchasing village land, giving up unwanted comforts etc.
  • Purchase village lands with the aim to move to simple life in future.
  • Understanding and taking up varnasrama lifestyle, etiquette, dress etc.
  • Helping Varnasrama Farms to purchase and setup village technology.
A few of the research services that can be taken up by VRT members:

Study and Research
  • Gathering information on the various topics available for research.
  • Attending seminars conducted by other agencies on topics related with varnasrama.
  • Conducting seminars to educate and train on topics related to varnasrama.
  • Enrolling and conducting e-courses.
Sharing
  • Writing articles on varnasrama and Krishna conscious topics in local newspapers or magazines.
  • Compiling materials such as presentations, pamphlets, brochures, newsletters, flyers, etc., for sharing the knowledge.
  • Help in building up online research repository by giving articles, reviews, suggestions, comments, proofreading, etc.
