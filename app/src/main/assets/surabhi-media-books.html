1. MAKE VRINDAVANA VILLAGES – In Support of Varnasrama Dharma

This small book, MAKE VRINDAVANA VILLAGES, was published in the year 2007, covering 5 Chapters and containing 75 pages, including close to 40 coloured photos depicting various rural scenes. The author introduces "ISKCON's Four Movements" in his first chapter, followed by "Quotes from Srila Prabhupada" in the second chapter, an article "Becoming Varnasrama Alert" by Sriman Tejiyas Das in the third chapter, an interview with the author, "Varnasrama Shift", in the fourth chapter and â€˜Vaisnava Village in Indonesia" in the last and final chapter. The book has been translated in the following international languages: French, Russian, Spanish and Bahasa Indonesia. It is also available in the following Indian local languages: Bengali, Oriya, Telegu, Hindi and Kanada. Foreword by Sriman Samba Das. Author: HH Bhakti Raghava Swami

2. VARNASRAMA EDUCATION – In Support of Traditional Education

The book VARNASRAMA EDUCATION was published in the year 2008 and is divided in two main parts: 1) Varnasrama Education – An Introductory Manual totalling 11 Chapters and 2) Traditional Education with 2 Chapters. Covering a total of 174 pages with some 20 coloured photos, the book attempts to introduce readers to some basic concepts and principles of the traditional system of education called gurukula. From the back cover:"We must understand that, according to Vedic culture, by definition and purpose, the systems of Varna, aptitude-based learning, and Asrama, phased lifelong education, are in themselves educational institutions". The book has been translated in Bahasa Indonesia. Foreword by HH Bhakti Vikasa Swami. Author: HH Bhakti Raghava Swami

3. IMPLEMENTING VARNASRAMA – GLOVESCO Reference Guide

The book IMPLEMENTING VARNASRAMA, GLOVESCO Reference Guide, was published in March, 2008 and spans over 12 Chapters for a total of 276 pages. The book is more or less a reprint of the material available on the website, www.glovesco.org which served at the time for propagating the varnasrama mission. The book includes a free multimedia CD. Foreword by Sriman Advaita Chandra Das. Author: HH Bhakti Raghava Swami

4. OVERHAUL AND REVERT – Promoting the Varnasrama Mission

This book, OVERHAUL AND REVERT – Promoting the Varnasrama Mission, was published in June, 2010 and assembles references on varnasrama from a course given on the Bhagavad-gita. The author presents many references from Srila Prabhupada, both from the Bhagavad-gita and the Srimad-Bhagavatam, underlying the need to bring about major changes in our present misdirected modern society and calls for a return to the more traditional lifestyle as advocated in the Vedic culture. Author: Sriman Raya Ramananda Das

5. THE FOURTH WAVE – A Collection of HH Bhakti Raghava Swami's Essays on Daiva Varnasrama

This small book, THE FOURTH WAVE, a collection of articles written by HH Bhakti Raghava Swami for the monthly newsletter THE EIGHT PETALS was published in June, 2010. The author has compiled 11 articles which cover the following topics: 1. Janmastami & the Varnasrama Mission; 2. Varnasrama...the Mission Impossible; 3. Traditional Education; 4. Vedic Technology, Arts & Crafts; 5. Leadership Crisis; 6. What is Dharma?; 7. Guiding Principles for Rural Communities or Villages; 8. The Sacred Oath: Protect our Sacred Mothers- the Cow & the Earth; 9. Village Life: Our Philosophy, Our Education, Our Lifestyle; 10. Grhastha Asrama: A Vedic Perspective and 11. Vedic Solution to Food Problems. Edited and compiled by Dr. Vrinda Baxi (Srimati Vrindavana Lila d.d.). Author: Dr. Vrinda Baxi
